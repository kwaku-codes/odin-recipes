Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry is a wonderful and staple dish of Asian cuisine. Though thought to be popular only in countries like India or Pakistan.

Curry is a dish that is beloved in other parts of Asia such as China and Japan both of which have specialised in their own version of curry.

This recipe is a traditional indian curry recipe commonly known as a chicken korma. It is well liked for its creamy yet spicy and flavourful taste.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast 500g
  • x 1 Jar Pataks korma Sauce
  • x 1 White Onion
  • x 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • x1 Scotch Bonnet
  • x1 Bell Pepper
  • x1 Double Cream
  • x1 Pack of Hot Madras Curry Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • All Purpose Seasoning

Cooking Step 1

  1. Wash your chicken with lemon and vinegar and disinfect your sink aftwerwards
  2. Dice your chciken into 1 inch cubes
  3. Season your chicken with 2 tbsp of soy sauce, 3 tsp of chicken stock powder or all purpose seasoning
  4. Mix till combined and then put to one side.

Cooking Step 2

  1. Dice up all your vegetables (onion, garlic, scoth bonnet & bell pepper)
  2. Add your onion and garlic to a heated pot with some oil and cook untile lightly brown.
  3. Once lightly browned add your chicken
  4. Dice scotch bonnet
  5. Once chicken has cooked half away add curry sauce and stir until chicken is fully coated
  6. Add 2-3 tablespoons of curry powder and stir fry until covered
