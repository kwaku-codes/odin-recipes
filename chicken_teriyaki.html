Chicken Teriyaki

Description

Chicken Teriyaki is a Japanese dish that consists of cooked chicken in a teryikai sauce, made from a combination of soy sauce, brown sugar and vinegar. There are variations in the recipes which allow for all sorts of condiments to be added to make the sauce slightly sweeter or sharp.

Teriyaki is a Japanese style of cooking using a glaze. The glaze is cooked until thick and then used to marinate the meat before cooking

You can buy it pre-made but this recipe will include steps on how you can make a quick version of this awesome glaze. Sure to make any meat delightfully delectable.

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken Breast
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 pack of spring onions
  • Seasme oil
  • Honey
  • Dark Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Cornflour
  • All purpose seasoing
  • Brown sugar

Making Teriyaki Sauce

To a jug add:

  • Add 5 tablespoons of dark soy sauce.
  • 3 tablesppons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of white wine vinegar (if you can get rice wine vinegar then use this instead)
  • 1 teaspoon of seasme oil
  • 1 inch of finely diced ginger
  • 1 teaspoon of honey
  • Feel free to add more honey or brown sugar if sauce tastes salty

Cooking Steps

  1. Dice your onion and garlic and put them in separate bowls. Additionally slice your green onion separating the white and green parts.
  2. Slice your chicken into 1 inch slices and put them in a clean bowl. (Wash with lemon and vinegar at your own descretion...please wash your chicken.)
  3. Once your chicken is in a separate bowl, marinate in a combination of spices and seasonings including all purpose seasoning, salt and pepper.
  4. Heat up your wok or pan on the stove, add oil and once hot add in your onion and garlic
  5. Once onion and garlic is brown add your chicken until brown and cooked through

    6. (Fun fact you can also coat your chicken in flour and fry in shallow oil in the pan this helps the sauce stick better to the chicken and taste amazing)

  6. Once chicken is cooked through add in teriyaki suace and sitr until entire chicken is coated
  7. Once sauce is thickened slightly garnish with green onions and serve with white rice
