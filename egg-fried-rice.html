Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is a staple dish that is beloved by all. A dish that originated in Yangzhou, China by peasants who created this dish to avoid wasting food and using leftovers. This dish was also the favourite of the Emperor Yang of the Sui Dyanasty

Today fried rice is a versatile dish that you can customise to your liking completely however you wish. This recipe will stick to the basics and focus on speed, and should be a dish you can make in 20mins to half an hour.

Ingredients

  • x 3 Eggs
  • x 5 Spring Onions
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • All Purpose Seaosoning
  • Day old cooked white rice

Cooking Steps

  1. Start by heating your pan up with some oil. And whisk/beat your eggs together in a bowl
  2. Dice up some onion and garlic and add them to your heated frying pan
  3. Once your eggs adn garlic are nice and brown push to one side in the pan and add in y our eggs and scramble until halfway cooked
  4. Add your rice and mix well crushing the rice as you do so it softens up
  5. Once all your ingredients are mixxed together add 2-3 tablespoons or dark soy suace and season with all purpose seasoning, salt and pepper and mix well
  6. Once rice is cooked serve on a plate with any desired sides or toppings
