Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is a staple dish that is beloved by all. A dish that originated in Yangzhou, China by peasants who created this dish to avoid wasting food and using leftovers. This dish was also the favourite of the Emperor Yang of the Sui Dyanasty

Today fried rice is a versatile dish that you can customise to your liking completely however you wish. This recipe will stick to the basics and focus on speed, and should be a dish you can make in 20mins to half an hour.

Ingredients

  • x 3 Eggs
  • x 5 Spring Onions
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • All Purpose Seaosoning
  • White Rice
